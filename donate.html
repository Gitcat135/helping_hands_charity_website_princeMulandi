Why Donate?

Every dollar you donate goes directly toward our programs that provide education to underprivileged children. Your support helps us:

  • Build and maintain schools in underserved areas
  • Train teachers to deliver quality education
  • Provide scholarships to deserving students
  • Supply books, uniforms, and learning materials
  • Create sustainable education programs
Helping Hands is a registered Non-governmental Organization (NGO). All donations are tax-exempt.
