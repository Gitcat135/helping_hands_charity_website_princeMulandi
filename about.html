About CarProtect

Our mission and team behind our work.

Our Story

At CarProtect, we believe that every journey should be safe and worry-free. Since 2010, we’ve been helping drivers across Kenya get the coverage they need with simple, clear policies and fast, reliable claims.

What started as a small team passionate about road safety has grown into a trusted insurance provider for families, individuals, and businesses. Our focus is always on protecting what matters most and giving our customers the confidence to drive with peace of mind.

At CarProtect, insurance isn’t just about policies—it’s about care, trust, and security on every journey.

Our mission

At CarProtect, our mission is to provide every driver in Kenya with reliable, simple, and affordable car insurance that protects what matters most. We aim to deliver fast claims, clear policies, and exceptional customer service, ensuring peace of mind on every journey.

Our vision

Our vision is to be the most trusted and innovative car insurance provider in Kenya, empowering drivers to feel confident and secure on the road, while setting the standard for excellence, transparency, and care in the insurance industry.

Our Values

Integrity

We conduct our business with honesty and transparency. Every policy, every claim, and every interaction reflects our commitment to doing the right thing for our customers.

Customer Focus

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We listen, understand, and provide solutions tailored to their needs, ensuring peace of mind on every journey.

Exellence

We strive for the highest standards in service, innovation, and expertise. From our skilled team to our streamlined processes, we are committed to delivering excellence in all we do.
